# Foxhole Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-05

## Data Collection

Foxhole does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data (session history, presets, streaks) is stored locally on your device.

## Permissions

- **Do Not Disturb access**: Used to silence notifications during focus sessions. Your previous DND state is restored when a session ends.
- **Foreground service**: Keeps the timer running accurately when the app is in the background.
- **Vibration**: Optional haptic feedback when a session completes.
- **Notifications**: Shows timer countdown and session completion alerts.

## Third-Party Services

Foxhole uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/hionnode/foxhole/issues.
